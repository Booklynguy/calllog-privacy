Privacy Policy for Dialyx

Last Updated: June 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how nexontech ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our mobile application, Dialyx (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our Service.

a) Information You Provide to Us:

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect authentication information. This may include your Google account details (name, email address, profile picture) or your phone number, depending on the sign-in method you choose.

User Content: We collect the data you create within the Service, such as call log generation presets, custom scenarios, and contact information you manually enter into the app.

b) Information We Access with Your Permission:

With your explicit consent, we access the following information on your device. We will not access this information without your permission.

  • Call Logs (via READ_CALL_LOG and WRITE_CALL_LOG permissions): Required to read your existing call history for display and management, and to write new call log entries that you generate using the Service.
  • Contacts (via READ_CONTACTS and WRITE_CONTACTS permissions): Required to allow you to select existing contacts for log generation and to create new contacts in your device's address book through the Service.

c) Information We May Collect in the Future (with permission):

If you choose to enable advanced features like context-aware generation, we may request permission to access your calendar or location data to provide these specific services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Service.
  • Authenticate your account and prevent fraud.
  • Enable core features like call log generation, preset management, and scenario execution.
  • Enable cloud backup and synchronization of your data (if you opt-in to this feature).
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., displaying your profile picture).
  • Communicate with you, including responding to your comments, questions, and requests.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers that perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting and user authentication. Our primary service provider is Google (Firebase) for authentication, cloud storage, and database services. You can review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. You can delete your account and associated data at any time from within the app's settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
